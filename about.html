About me

My name is Lillian Muita a nature enthusiast and lover of music plus cuisines from other cultures.
Ever since I can remember I have always wanted to change the lives of people in my community especially the vulnerable. I believe we were all put on earth for a reason; to help each other. It was this passion for community development that pushed me to pursue International Relation Studies as i was seeking answers to better my world. This remains my goal to this day and one of the biggest contributors why I decided to be a software developer. If the events from the last two years have taught us anything, it's that there is a strong need for an international perspective and a more global vision, to better understand the conflicts and challenges surrounding us. One major consequence of the pandemic was a blow to traditional ways of doing things that we were accustomed to. How then can we remain relevant? How else can we impact our world? How else can we develop? I chose to code. Through coding I can continue to be part of the solution to the ever evolving now digital globe.
